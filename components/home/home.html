About Me

Welcome to my site! I'm a computing enthusiast interested in all facets of the industry from silicon to operating systems to web technologies to machine learning to graphics.

I recently graduated from Ryerson University and am now employed SS&C as a software developer where I am learning to work with VueJS, as well as Selenium through the Java API.

My latest projects involve extensive research into operating system design, starting from older systems like MS-DOS and Version 7 Unix. The aim is to construct a simple operating system that is compliant with modern standards, while being as backwards-compatible as possible.
